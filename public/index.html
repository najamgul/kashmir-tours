✦ Day-by-Day Plan

Golden Kashmir Tour Itinerary

A detailed walkthrough of our most popular 7-day journey through Kashmir's finest destinations.

01

Arrival in Srinagar

Welcome to Paradise

Arrive at Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport. Transfer to your heritage houseboat on Dal Lake. Evening Shikara ride through floating gardens and the old city waterways. Welcome Kahwa and Kashmiri snacks on-board.

02

Srinagar – Mughal Heritage

Gardens & Culture

Full day tour of Mughal Gardens — Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Cheshmashahi. Visit Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic valley views. Evening free for shopping in the old city bazaars for pashmina and walnut woodcraft.

03

Srinagar → Gulmarg

Meadow of Flowers

Drive to Gulmarg (2,650m). Ride the Gulmarg Gondola — one of the highest cable cars in the world. Enjoy panoramic views of Nanga Parbat and the Pir Panjal range. Optional skiing/snowboarding in season. Overnight in Gulmarg.

04

Gulmarg → Pahalgam

Valley of Shepherds

Scenic drive to Pahalgam via charming Kashmiri villages and saffron fields. Visit the confluence of Lidder and Sheshnag rivers. Evening stroll along Lidder River amidst pine groves and wildflower meadows.

05

Pahalgam – Valley Excursions

Betaab & Aru Valley

Full day exploring Betaab Valley (Bollywood's favourite!), Chandanwari — the starting point of Amarnath Yatra, and the pristine Aru Valley. Optional pony ride through the alpine meadows. Bonfire evening with Kashmiri music.

06

Pahalgam → Sonamarg

Meadow of Gold

Journey to Sonamarg through dramatic mountain passes. Trek to Thajiwas Glacier — a majestic wall of ice surrounded by wildflowers. Picnic lunch by the Sind River. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

07

Departure Day

Farewell, Kashmir

Early morning Shikara ride for sunrise over the Himalayas. Leisurely breakfast. Shopping for souvenirs — saffron, dry fruits, and kashmiri shawls. Transfer to airport. Depart with a heart full of golden memories. ❤️

✦ Culinary Heritage

Savour the Legendary Wazwan

Kashmir's cuisine is an art form perfected over centuries. The Wazwan — a multi-course feast of 36 dishes — is the crown jewel of Kashmiri hospitality. From the aromatic Rogan Josh to the delicate Gushtaba, every bite tells a story of Mughal emperors and mountain spice routes.

Our packages include authentic dining experiences with local families, cooking masterclasses, and visits to the famed saffron fields of Pampore.
